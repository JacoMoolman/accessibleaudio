# Accessible Audio voice-sample dialogues

Status: review scripts only. Do **not** generate audio from these scripts until
the issue owner approves them.

The current source lists contain 5 local favourite voices and 30 Gemini Text to
Speech voices: 35 voices and therefore 35 distinct two-person dialogues.

## 01 — Local: Woman Kid Story — Frustration to relief

**Lina:** I kept missing the class story because there was no audio version.

**Sam:** That is exactly why Accessible Audio turns ready text into an audiobook. You should not have to sit out the adventure.

## 02 — Local: Male One — Discovery and excitement

**Theo:** You mean one book can speak in English and then in isiZulu too?

**Ayo:** Yes. Accessible Audio can prepare language editions so more listeners can meet the same story in a voice they understand.

## 03 — Local: Woman One — Pride and warmth

**Kea:** Then the book finally reached the person it was written for. That is a wonderful reason to make audio accessible.

## 04 — Local: Woman Two — Worry to reassurance

## 05 — Local: Male_smooth — Calm empathy

## 06 — Gemini: Zephyr — Urgency
